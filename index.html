About

From where our journey began?

The CSE Active group was created on January 30th, 2021. The day was stressful, the students had an exam of Mechanical Workshop in the morning. The preparation was not very good. So, all students of CSE department 1st year decided to unite and use the discord server of their department for discussion. The plan went well and they finished their exam with full marks. But unfortunately their were some imposters among us. They told the teacher about our plan and he was furios upon us. It was then that a handful of students from the CSE branch of KGEC came together and decided to create their own group. After that the students decided to hold on to that bond and help each other in all situations henceforth...
